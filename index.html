Product Description

Noswet Roll ON Spray is an anti-perspirant which contains Aluminium Chloride as the active ingredient. It temporarily suppresses the flow of sweat and controls excessive sweating and bad odour

Key Benefits

  • Used to control excessive sweating
  • The metal ions precipitate with mucopolysaccharides
  • Damages the epithelial cells along the lumen of the duct and forms a plug that blocks sweat output
  • Has an astringent action on the skin cells, which results in inhibition of sweating
  • Improves body odour

Key Ingredients

Aluminium Chloride Hexahydrate

Works by partially blocking the sweat glands
Leaves the skin dry and without any compensatory sweating

Dimethicone Copolyol

Gentle emollient action
Water repellent. more water-proof form of dimethicone that adheres better to skin and hair

Directions for Use

  • Noswet should only be applied to your skin
  • Best to use Noswet at night before going to sleep when the sweat glands are least active
  • Wash off in the morning and do not re-apply during the day

Safety Information

  • Read the label carefully before use
  • Do not exceed the recommended dose
  • Keep out of reach and sight of children
  • Store the formulation in cool and dry place
